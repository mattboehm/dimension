Buyers earn tokens by buying objects from sellers and then exchanging them for tokens.

When you are a buyer, your only task is to choose the seller you want to buy from.

{% if subsession.dims == 1 %}

After a buyer chooses a seller, the buyer pays the seller the seller's price.

{% endif %}

To the right is an example buyer interface. Feel free to experiment with choosing a seller there.

{% if subsession.dims > 1 %}

As you can see, buyers do not see the sellers’ total prices, but they do see their sub-prices.

After a buyer chooses a seller, the buyer pays the seller the sum of the seller’s sub-prices (the total price).

{% endif %}

After the buyer buys an object, the buyer will automatically exchange the object with the experimenter for 800 tokens.

Other than the price, it does not matter which seller the buyers buy from – they will always get 800 tokens at the end of the round.

Before continuing, please choose a seller in the example interface to the right.
