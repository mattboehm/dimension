True or False: Buyers earn fewer tokens if they pay a higher price.

Correct!

The correct answer is: True.

Buyers will always earn more tokens if they pay a lower price.

True or False: Sellers earn more tokens if they set a higher price.

Correct!

The correct answer is: It depends.

Since there are two sellers in each group, there is no guarantee that a seller will sell an object — a higher price may lead a buyer to choose from the other seller.

However, if a seller does sell an object, then a higher price will earn more tokens.
