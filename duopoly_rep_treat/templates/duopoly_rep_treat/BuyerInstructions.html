Imagine that you are playing as a buyer. You will buy one object per match.

First, you will see the {{ subsession.dims }} price{{ subsession.dims |pluralize }} that the sellers set for their objects, and be asked which of the {{ Constants.sellers_per_group }} sellers you want to buy from. You will then pay the seller you chose the sum of the {{ subsession.dims }} price{{ subsession.dims |pluralize }} that the seller set for their object.

The objects sold by the two sellers are identical. The only differences are the prices. No matter which seller you choose, you will be awarded {{ Constants.consbenefit }} tokens in exchange for your object at the end of the match. The sum of each seller's prices will always be less than or equal to {{ Constants.maxprice }} tokens.

An example of how you choose between sellers is shown in the table to the right. Note, that as the buyer you do not see the sum of the prices. You only see the {{ subsession.dims }} price{{ subsession.dims |pluralize }} themselves.

Please choose a seller in the example table. When you are done, press the "Next" button to continue.
